Curry

Description

Easy to make curry recipe. Golden Curry roux can be found in Asian aisle of most grocery stores.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups uncooked white sticky rice
  • 1 Box Extra Hot Golden Curry roux
  • 1 Large can black olives
  • 6 Medium-small potatoes
  • 4 Tbsp onion powder
  • 3 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 6 Boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 Large can diced pineapple
  • 1 Cup thinly sliced raw carrots
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pack shredded mexican-blend cheese

Steps:

  1. Prepare rice in rice cooker, according to cooker manufaturer's instructions
  2. Slice potatoes into thirds
  3. Slice each chicken thigh into 4-5 pieces
  4. Place thigh pieces into large pot and coat with olive oil
  5. Toss in onion and garlic powder, and mix until evenly coated
  6. Fry inside pot until outside of chicken is lightly brown (usually 10 mins)
  7. Add 2.5 cups water and bring to a low, simmering boil
  8. Toss in potatoes and stir, then cover the pot with lid
  9. After 10 mins of gentle boiling, turn off heat source and add roux
  10. THOROUGHLY stir in roux until COMPLETELY dissolved and even
  11. Toss in olives and stir
  12. Turn heat source back on and bring back to low, simmering boil
  13. Continuously (but gently) stir uncovered for 5 mins
  14. Check potato softness. Continue stirring until desired softness achieved
  15. Toss in sliced carrots and mix, then immediately remove pot from heat source before carrots can turn soft
  16. Scoop desired amount of rice into a plate, ensuring rice only takes up half the plate
  17. Fill remaining half of plate with roux mixture
  18. Sprinkle shredded cheese over line separating rice from roux
  19. Place pineapple into small side dish
  20. Enjoy, while aternating between spicy curry and cooling pineapple.
