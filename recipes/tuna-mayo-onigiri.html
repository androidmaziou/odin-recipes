Tuna Mayo Onigiri

Description

A quick but tasty lunch dish. Good substitute for the same, boring sandwich you normally prepare, when in a hurry.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup uncooked white sticky rice
  • 1 Large packet tuna in water (drained, no oil, never albacore)
  • 3 Tsp mayo
  • Sea salt shaker
  • Packet of nori seaweed slices

Steps:

  1. Prepare rice in rice cooker according to cooker manufacturer's instructions
  2. Mix tuna and mayo together in small mixing bowl
  3. Lay out nori slices onto dry dish
  4. When rice is finished cooking, run your left hand under cold water
  5. Shake sea salt onto wet hand until very lightly coated
  6. Scoop desired amount of rice onto wet, salty hand and form disc shape
  7. Using a teaspoon, scoop small dab of tuna from bowl into middle of rice in hand
  8. Fold rice over tuna dab and form into triangle shape
  9. Place rice triangle onto middle of a nori slice so base is on nori and upper tip is point to ceiling
  10. Wrap nori against the sides of the rice triangle
  11. Scoop out another very small dab of tuna and place gently on upper tip of triangle
  12. Repeat until desired number of onigiri triangles achieved
