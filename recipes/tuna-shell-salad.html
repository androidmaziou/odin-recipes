Tuna Shell Salad

Description

A simple but tasty comfort dish. All ingredients are raw, and mixed together in a large mixing bowl.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Box of tuna shells
  • 4 Large packets tuna in water (drained, never oil, never albacore)
  • 1 Large can sweet peas (drained)
  • 2 Large cans black olives (drained)
  • 1 Cucumber (diced)
  • 2 Tbsp Dill pickle juice
  • Enough Miracle Whip to completely coat the entire mixture
  • Yellow mustard to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil tuna shells until soft (typically 7-10 mins depending on water temp and preference)
  2. While waiting for shells to boil, place tuna, peas, olives, diced cucumber into large mixing bowl
  3. Drain boiled shells in colander and cool down with room-temp water
  4. Gently shake cooled shells in colander to drain excess water
  5. Once excess water is gone, dump shells into mixing bowl with rest of the ingredients
  6. Add pickle juice
  7. Add three heaping tablespoons of Miracle Whip and mix
  8. Continue adding Miracle Whip until desired consistency achieved
  9. Add 2 tsp yellow mustard and mix. Continue adding mustard until just slightly tangy. Mustard flavor should be barey noticeable
